Philosophy
01

People remember feelings, not floor plans.

We design each room with a single clear atmosphere — then remove anything that doesn't serve that mood. The result is a space that feels intentional.

We care about materials, light, and quiet details — the kind you notice slowly. The texture of a linen pillow. The way afternoon light moves across concrete.

Another House is not trying to be everything. It is trying to be exactly one thing, done very well.
